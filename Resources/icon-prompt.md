A macOS app icon for a disk cleaner utility called "ClearFile".

Design requirements:
- macOS Big Sur style: rounded super-ellipse (squircle) shape
- Single centered icon graphic, no text, no letters
- Clean, minimal, modern design
- Main element: a stylized hard drive or disk with a sparkle/shine effect, symbolizing cleaning
- Color: blue gradient (#007AFF to #0050AB), with white/light accents
- A small broom or sparkle star accent in the corner to indicate "cleaning"
- Subtle shadow and depth (3D-ish but not overdone)
- Solid colored background within the squircle shape
- No busy details, should be recognizable at 16x16 pixels
- Professional utility app aesthetic, similar to DaisyDisk or CleanMyMac icon quality
- Output: 1024x1024 pixels, PNG with transparent background outside the squircle
